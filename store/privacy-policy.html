Whatnow Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Overview

Whatnow ("we", "our", "the app") is a task management application designed to help users with ADHD and task paralysis. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and display name via Supabase Authentication (which supports Google Sign-In and email/password).

Task data: Tasks you create, including names, descriptions, types, time estimates, and completion statistics. This data is stored securely in our Supabase database.

Usage data: Points earned, tasks completed, time spent, and rank progression to power the gamification features.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the app's core functionality
  • To sync your tasks across devices
  • To calculate leaderboard rankings within groups you join
  • To process premium subscriptions via Stripe

Third-Party Services

Supabase: Backend database and authentication. Supabase Privacy Policy

Google AdMob: We display ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. Google Privacy Policy

Stripe: Payment processing for premium subscriptions. We do not store your payment details. Stripe Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Your data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure. Task data may also be cached locally on your device for offline access using an encrypted local database.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with the third-party services listed above as necessary to provide the app's functionality.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and export your data at any time through the app
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

Children's Privacy

Whatnow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
