## Full Description
Stuck staring at your to-do list, overwhelmed by everything you need to do? Whatnow breaks the cycle of task paralysis.

★ SWIPE TO DECIDE
Whatnow shows you one task at a time. Swipe right to commit, swipe left to skip. No more agonising over what to do first — just pick and go.

★ BUILT FOR ADHD BRAINS
Every feature is designed to reduce decision fatigue:
• Filter tasks by time available, energy level, and social battery
• Quick task import — paste a list and go
• Recurring tasks for daily routines
• Timed focus sessions with celebration rewards

★ GAMIFIED MOTIVATION
• Earn points for every task you complete
• Climb through 9 ranks from Task Newbie to Legend
• Compete with friends on weekly leaderboards
• Join groups to stay accountable together

★ WORKS OFFLINE
Your tasks sync automatically when you reconnect. Use Whatnow anywhere, anytime.

★ PREMIUM (OPTIONAL)
• Unlimited tasks (free plan: 20 tasks)
• Ad-free experience
• Priority support

Whatnow is not just another to-do app. It's designed specifically for people who know what they need to do but can't get started. If executive dysfunction is your nemesis, Whatnow is your sidekick.

Stop overthinking. Start doing.
