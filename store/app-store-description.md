## Description
Stuck staring at your to-do list, overwhelmed by everything you need to do? Whatnow breaks the cycle.

**Swipe to decide.** Whatnow shows you one task at a time. Swipe right to commit, swipe left to skip. No more agonising over what to do first.

**Built for ADHD brains.** Every feature is designed to reduce decision fatigue:
- Filter tasks by time, energy, and social battery
- Quick task import — paste a list, set defaults, done
- Recurring tasks for routines you want to stick to
- Timed focus sessions with celebration rewards

**Gamified motivation.**
- Earn points for every task you complete
- Climb through 9 ranks from Task Newbie to Legend
- Compete with friends on weekly leaderboards
- Join groups to stay accountable

**Works offline.** Your tasks sync when you're back online. No internet? No problem.

**Premium unlocks:**
- Unlimited tasks (free: 20 tasks)
- Ad-free experience
- Priority support

Stop overthinking. Start doing. Download Whatnow.
